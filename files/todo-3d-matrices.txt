You need add standard matrixes: Projection, View, Model.

In tri-data.yaml, i added `scene`, and `cameras`.

Now program must draw only figures from scene. And need to use camera from `scene`.

Camera contains two vectors: forward, up.

The length of the forward vector is not necessarily equal to one—it needs to be normalized.
The up vector does not necessarily need to be perpendicular to the forward vector.
We first need to obtain the vector `left = up * forward`. Then `up2 = forward * left`.
And `up2` will be perpendicular to forward. And then we need to normalize `forward` and `up2`.

And then from the normalized vectors `forward` and `up2` and from `fov` construct matrices.

FOV is specified in degrees.
